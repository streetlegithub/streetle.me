RGB Hotbars

RGB Hotbars is a unique and colorful Minecraft mod that offers a fun way to customize your gameplay experience. With this mod, players have the ability to change the color of their health bar hearts to any color they desire, allowing them to personalize their hotbar and make it stand out from the rest. Whether you prefer a dark blue or a bright pink look, RGB Hotbars gives you the freedom to express your creativity and make your Minecraft world truly your own. Install the mod today and enjoy a vibrant new addition to your gameplay.

The RGB Hotbars Minecraft mod offers a variety of exciting features that allow players to customize the color of their health bar hearts. With this mod, users have the ability to choose from a wide range of colors to match their personal style. Overall, the RGB Hotbars mod provides a fun and creative way to personalize your Minecraft gameplay and make your hotbar stand out from the rest.

Windows 10 PCs

Download the file below. Click on the rgb-hotbars.mcpack file and the Minecraft Application will launch, upon launching, allow 10-15 seconds for the mod to finish importing. Then, enable the mod in "Global Resources" in your settings or "Resource Packs" to enable it in specific worlds.

This mod will work on any device that can run Minecraft Bedrock Edition and install mods.

This mod will be compatible with any mod that does not change the hotbar textures.

How do I change the colors?

Press the settings icon once the mod is activated.

Why won't the colors change?

Try restarting your game. If this still doesn't work, turn the mod off and back on.

Why does the mod fail to import?

Try changing the file extension to zip, then uncompress it and put it in your resource_packs folder. This may work however is not recommended and should only be used as a final option.

Is there a rainbow option?

Currently no, however it may be added in the future.
