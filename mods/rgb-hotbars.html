RGB Hotbars

RGB Hotbars is a unique and colorful Minecraft mod that offers a fun way to customize your gameplay experience. With this mod, players have the ability to change the color of their health bar hearts to any color they desire, allowing them to personalize their hotbar and make it stand out from the rest. Whether you prefer a rainbow of colors or a sleek monochromatic look, RGB Hotbars gives you the freedom to express your creativity and make your Minecraft world truly your own. Install the mod today and enjoy a vibrant new addition to your gameplay.

The RGB Hotbars Minecraft mod offers a variety of exciting features that allow players to customize the color of their health bar hearts. With this mod, users have the ability to choose from a wide range of colors to match their personal style. Overall, the RGB Hotbars mod provides a fun and creative way to personalize your Minecraft gameplay and make your hotbar stand out from the rest.

Windows 10 PCs

Download the file below. Click on the rgb-hotbars.mcpack file and the Minecraft Application will launch, upon launching, allow 10-15 seconds for the mod to finish importing. Then, enable the mod in "Global Resources" in your settings or "Resource Packs" to enable it in specific worlds.
