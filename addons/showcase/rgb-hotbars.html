RGB Hotbars

Intro

RGB Hotbars is a unique and colourful Minecraft addon that offers a fun way to customize your gameplay experience. With this addon, players have the ability to change the colour of their health bar hearts to any colour they desire, allowing them to personalize their hotbar and make it stand out from the rest. Whether you prefer a dark blue or a bright pink look, RGB Hotbars gives you the freedom to express your creativity and make your Minecraft world truly your own. Install the addon today and enjoy a vibrant new addition to your gameplay.

Instructions

To change the colour please press the settings icon next to "Deactivate"
