RGB Hearts

Intro

RGB Hearts is a must-have addon for Minecraft enthusiasts who love to customize their gaming experience. With this addon, players have the power to choose the colour of their hotbar from a wide range of vibrant hues. The addon is easy to use and allows for the ultimate customization options, allowing players to express their unique style and personality in the game. Whether you prefer a classic red, a soothing blue, or a bold neon green, RGB Hearts has got you covered.

Instructions

To change the colour please press the settings icon next to "Deactivate"
