Glass Shields

Intro

Do you have trouble seeing players when you PvP? This mod will help you focus on the real gameplay, while still letting you know you have a shield equipped. You can customise your shield from a wide range of colors. The shield will always be glass, as to be slightly translucent and not block your view.

Instructions

To change the colour please press the settings icon next to "Deactivate"
