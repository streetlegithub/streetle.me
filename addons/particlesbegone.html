Particles Be Gone


Is your game too laggy and you can't even play properly due to low FPS? Well, this addon can help with that!

I tried this before, however the addon merely made the particles a blank texture, they were still technically being rendered.
I have now entirely re-made the addon to make the particles never even spawn in. You will never see another particle again with this addon enabled.


A GIF of the addon in action. As you can see, there are no block destruct particles


Here is a sheep absolutely getting it. There are no critical hit particles anywhere. They just don't exist.



This addon is Mobile, Console and PC friendly.
