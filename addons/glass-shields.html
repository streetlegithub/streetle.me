Glass Shields


With this Minecraft Mod, you can customise your UI like no other! Whether you're playing survival with your friends or doing some PVP on a server, this mod will help you focus on the real gameplay, while still knowing you have a shield equipped.

You can customise your shield from a wide range of colors. The shield will always be glass, as to be slightly translucent and not block your view.

How do I change the color?

When the pack is activated, there should be a settings icon when you click on it.






This addon is Mobile, Console and PC friendly.
