Lower Swords


Have you ever been playing Minecraft and realised... these swords are too damn big! They take up a lot of your screen. This can be especially bad in PvP as you won't be able to see who is sneaking up on you.
With this addon, all your problems are solved. Whether you're on a singleplayer world, a multiplayer world with friends, or even a server!


No animals were harmed in the making of these screenshots.



This addon is Mobile, Console and PC friendly.
