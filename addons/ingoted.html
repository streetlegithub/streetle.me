Ingoted


Ingoted makes your Minecraft experience a lot more finished and allows you to have better understanding of what items are made from ores and what aren't. This helps make all mineable ores into ingots once smelted. It can also work on servers such as The Hive or Cubecraft, as they use smelted ores as currency.
This mod changes Diamonds, Emeralds and Coal into ingots, as it should be!




This addon is Mobile, Console and PC friendly.
