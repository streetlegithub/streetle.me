That Night at The Market (DEMO)

You go on a long walk to try and visit an old friend. You can't walk all the way so decide to rent a place for a couple nights.
You do however need supplies like food. You need to stop off at The Market. Its open 24/7. You never see the workers go home. You never see anyone go home.

Uncover the mysterious past of this town.

IMPORTANT INFO, PLEASE READ

This version of this map is just a demo. The full version will be coming out (hopefully) sometime in August.
This version is just a small little sneak peak of whats to come in the final release, aswell as a kind of beta test for people to report any bugs they find.
The demo will not be updated unless it is a major game-breaking bug. The final release will be much more fleshed out.

This world requires 1.19.80+ to function correctly.

This world is Mobile, Console and PC friendly.
