That Night at The Market

You go on a long walk to try and visit an old friend. You can't walk all the way so decide to rent a place for a couple nights. You need supplies though like food. You need to stop off at The Market. Its open 24/7. You never see the workers go home. You never see anyone go home. Uncover the mysterious past of this town.

This world requires 1.19.90+ to function correctly.
